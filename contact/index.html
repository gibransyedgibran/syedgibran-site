you made it here

You don't need
a reason.

You opened this page. That already means something. Most people read and leave. You stayed long enough to wonder if there was a way to reach the person behind the words.

Before you talk yourself out of it — and you are already talking yourself out of it — I want you to know something.

You don't need a question worth asking or an insight worth sharing or a problem important enough to justify the message. You don't need to have read everything or figured out something profound or have anything to offer in return.

You can just say hi.

Tell me about your week. Tell me where you are in the rebuild. Tell me what is crumbling right now or what just started to hold. Tell me you are still showing up even though nothing has moved yet.

That is enough. That is more than enough.

I read everything. It is just me on the other side of this.
